Clinimetrics
Clinimetrics can be broadly defined as the science of clinical measurements.
The study for assessing the quality of measurements for clinical research and practice in healthcare.
The term refers to Measurement in Medicine and is about making health data.
This concept refers to a set of principles and rules that clinicians use to improve the treatment of patients.
We use this measure the severity of symptoms, signs, and laboratory findings by means of quantitative approaches to assessment.
Metrology
The International Bureau of Weights and Measures defines metrology as the science of measurement, embracing both experimental and theoretical determinations at any level of uncertainty in any field of science and technology.
Measurements of physiological, biochemical, physical, and other patient-related variables provide essential information for critical decision-making in clinical practice, as well as for research and development.
